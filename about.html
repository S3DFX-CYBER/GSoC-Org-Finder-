About FindMyGSoC

Helping students discover the perfect Google Summer of Code organization.

What is FindMyGSoC?

FindMyGSoC is a platform that helps students discover Google Summer of Code organizations, explore technologies, find beginner-friendly issues, connect with mentors, and identify organizations that best match their skills.

Key Features

🤖 AI Recommender

Get personalized organization recommendations based on your skills.

⭐ Watchlist

Save organizations and track them throughout the application process.

🐛 Good First Issues

Find beginner-friendly issues to start contributing immediately.

👨‍🏫 Mentor Finder

Explore mentors and organization contacts.

📅 Timeline

Track important GSoC dates and milestones.

🎲 Surprise Me

Discover organizations randomly and explore new opportunities.
