Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

FindMyGSoC is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

1. Information We Collect

FindMyGSoC is a fully static, client-side web application. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers. All functionality (searching, filtering, and bookmarking) occurs locally in your browser.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser’s local storage only for:

  • Saving your organization Watchlist / Bookmarks
  • Remembering your theme preference (Light / Dark mode)

This data remains entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services & Technical Data

When you load the website, the following third-party services may receive standard technical metadata (such as your IP address, browser type, and referring page):

  • Google Fonts – Typography (Privacy Policy)
  • Tailwind CSS CDN – Styling framework
  • Vercel (or your hosting provider) – Static file delivery

4. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete your locally stored bookmarks
  • Clear all local storage data through your browser settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

5. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the integrity of our static site. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

privacy@findmygsoc.vercel.app

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date.
